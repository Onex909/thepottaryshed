About the Pottery Shed

The Pottery Shed was conceived in 2010 and established by American pottery teacher Joe Darling. The studio and the classes are beautifully organised to achieve the best success. It is always open so please drop in to look at it yourself.

We have training levels to suit raw beginners and intermediate levels. We fire to cone 03 for the lowest energy footprint possible and the brightest colors available. Other firing services include midfire temperatures.

For the beginner, we offer an excellent course in 3 classes and a special discounted 6-Pack of 3 classes and 3 practice sessions. Over the two hours, each class is divided similarly with an introduction, a demonstration, then a hands-on session with the clay and taking it step-by-step. The instructors will explain the purpose and principles behind the lesson.
